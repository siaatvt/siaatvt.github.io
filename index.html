What is SIA?

The Society of Indian Americans is a fun-filled organization that integrates Indian culture to our community at Virginia Tech! Through year-round events like SIA Show, Banquet, and Holi, we make sure that our members come out with great friendships and ever-lasting memories! You don't have to be Indian to be a part, so join our community today!

Become A Member Today

Welcome to the Society of Indian Americans at Virginia Tech! Interested in becoming a member? During the beginning of the semester a google form will be posted with instructions on how to join this amazing organization. Furthermore, there will be a small membership fee associated with the application and once you have paid through cash or venmo you will be a member of SIA at VT! Being a member of the organization offers you several opportunities to meet your fellow Indian Americans at Virginia Tech as well as explore the activities/events SIA has to offer. Additionally, if you are interested in becoming a big or little the organization will pair you up with 3-4 other members to create an SIA family. Each SIA fam will fall under one of the three main families which are based off of luxury car brands: Ferrari fam, Lamborghini fam, and Bugatti fam.
